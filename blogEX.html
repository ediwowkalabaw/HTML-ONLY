Crowd estimate at start of Alyansa’s miting de avance reaches 22,000

By: Charie Abarca - Reporter

Published: 2025-05-09

MANILA, Philippines — The Alyansa Para sa Bagong Pilipinas said the crowd estimate at its Miting de Avance in Mandaluyong City has breached 20,000 as of 6 p.m. on Friday.

The crowd was estimated to be around 22,000, according to the Alyansa’s official social media page.

The Miting de Avance is being held at the Mandaluyong City Sports Complex. The event is expected to be attended by the party’s senatorial candidates and supporters.

The Alyansa is a coalition of various political parties and groups that aims to promote good governance and transparency in the Philippines. The group is led by its presidential candidate, former senator Ferdinand “Bongbong” Marcos Jr., and his running mate, Davao City Mayor Sara Duterte-Carpio.
